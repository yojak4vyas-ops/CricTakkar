Cricket Wordle
Guess the mystery cricketer — play as many rounds as you like, a new player every time.
📖 How to Play
1
Type any cricketer's name in the box and submit your guess.
2
After each guess, you see 8 coloured tiles — one for each attribute of that player.
3
Use the colours as clues to narrow down who the mystery player is.
4
Get all 8 tiles green = you found the player! You have 6 attempts total.
5
Share your coloured tile result on WhatsApp and challenge your friends!
🎨 What the Colours Mean
🟩
Green — Exact match. Your guess and the mystery player share this attribute exactly.
🟨
Yellow — Close match. Debut year within 5 years, ICC Trophies off by 1, shares at least one IPL team without matching exactly, or the two players share at least one format without matching exactly (e.g. one played ODI+T20I and the other played only T20I).
⬛
Grey — No match. This attribute is completely different from the mystery player.
🏏 The 8 Clues You Get After Each Guess
Country India / Australia / Pakistan…
Role Batsman / Bowler / All-rounder / Wicketkeeper
Bat Style Right-hand / Left-hand
Bowl Style e.g. Right-arm fast — NA if they never bowl
Debut Year e.g. 2008 — yellow if within 5 years
Format Shows exactly which formats they've played: Test, ODI, T20I, or a combo like Test+ODI, ODI+T20, or All-format
IPL Teams Every team they've played for — yellow if you share at least one
ICC Trophies How many ICC trophies they've won
